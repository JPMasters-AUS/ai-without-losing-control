# [DEC-XXX] [Decision Title]

> [!NOTE]
> A decision record captures why a material decision was made, not just what was decided. The rationale and alternatives considered are the most valuable parts of this record. Without them, future team members (or AI tools reviewing the project) have no way to understand the thinking behind the decision, and may inadvertently reverse it.
